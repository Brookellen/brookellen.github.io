Should I Say That?

The Mission

In the wake of the #MeToo movement, amidst countless stories of abuse and cries for change, a distinct and disturbing voice arose:



“So I can’t even hit on a woman anymore?”

Men were feeling nervous! Afraid! Targeted! We could’t stand idly by and allow them be put through such agony, so we came up with the idea to create a simple resource that aims to teach them the basics of interfacing with women without being aggressive, demeaning, and spiteful. We’re happy with the result and hopefully you are, too.

P.S. If this website has made you feel targeted,  maybe you should try smiling.
