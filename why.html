Should I Say That?

The Mission

In the wake of the #MeToo movement, amidst countless stories of abuse and cries for change, one voice rose clear above the rest:



“So I can’t even hit on a woman anymore?”

Men were feeling nervous, afraid, targeted! Not wanting to turn a blind eye to such injustice, we came up with the idea to create a simple resource that aims to teach them the basics of interfacing with women without being aggressive, demeaning, and spiteful. We’re happy with the result and hopefully you are, too.

P.S. If this website has made you feel uncomfortable,  maybe you should try smiling.
